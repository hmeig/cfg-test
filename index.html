About Us

The Daily Bread was created in 2016 to deliver freshly baked bread to busy people in the Birmingham area.

We know that everyone loves fresh bread but with busy schedules, it can be hard to find the time to buy it every morning.

So we have decided to help the people of Birmingham by delivering delicious, baked goods straight out of the oven to your front door!

All you need to do is fill out our order form with your preferred type of bread to be delivered, what days you'd like it and your delivery address.

Every morning, rain or shine, wake up to fresh bread on your doorstep!

Delivery Information

We will deliver your freshly baked bread in the morning between 7am and 9am.

Delivery costs £1 per order for fresh bread delivered to your door. We also offer free delivery if you spend over £10, just add the discount code "FREEDELIVERY" at checkout.
